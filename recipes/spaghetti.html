Spaghetti

Description

An easy homemade, baked spaghetti to make for dinner.

Ingredients

  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1 cup shredded mild Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Cook ground beef in hot skillet
  3. Boil spaghetti noodles in large pot
  4. Add spaghetti and ground beef in baking dish
  5. Bake for 30 minutes
  6. Serve and enjoy!
